Driven by science and data analytics.

Perform Training is a science and data analytics-backed sports performance training system created to help you achieve your athletic potential.

Each athlete invited to join Perform Training is evaluated based on 11 performance attributes key to any athlete’s success.

From these attribute scores, the Perform Team creates a comprehensive performance profile, or what we call an athlete's archetype.

ATTRIBUTES:

Power Speed Relative Strength Absolute Strength Body Composition Endurance Stamina Flexibility Balance Coordination Agility

Guided By Your Needs As An Athlete

After analyzing performance data from thousands of athletes at every level, we can identify and compare your current archetype with your ideal archetype based on your height, weight, sport, and position.
